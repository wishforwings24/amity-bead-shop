Our Process

Every Amity bracelet begins with a sketch and a curated selection of beads. We source our materials from ethical suppliers, focusing on glass, natural stone, and durable elastic.

Our process is slow and intentional. Each bead is hand-threaded, and every knot is double-secured to ensure your jewellery lasts through all of life's adventures. We test every design for comfort and durability before it ever reaches the shop.
