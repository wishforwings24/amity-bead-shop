Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to receive my order?

As all of our pieces are handmade to order, please allow 3-5 business days for processing. Once shipped, domestic orders typically arrive within 2-4 business days.

What materials do you use?

We use a variety of high-quality materials including faceted glass beads, natural gemstones, durable elastic cord, and stainless steel or 14k gold-filled hardware. Check individual product descriptions for specific material details.

How do I care for my jewelry?

To keep your bracelets looking their best, we recommend rolling them onto your wrist rather than stretching. Avoid contact with water, perfumes, and lotions. Store in a cool, dry place when not in use.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes! We love creating unique pieces. Please contact us via our contact form or DM us on Instagram to discuss your custom project.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If for any reason you're not satisfied, we accept returns on unworn items within 14 days of delivery. Please contact us to initiate a return.
